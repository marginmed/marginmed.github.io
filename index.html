Welcome to Marginmed

Motivation

People have rights to monitor their health data and we want to help them doing so.

Mission

We create technology to improve health status of the lower socio economic people. To ensure, protect and promote health by enabling access of self care device for the people form lower socio economic background

Vision

Serving the unprivileged people from every corner of the world with a comprehensive digital health assessment platform. To reach out world's poorer segment with an easy-to-use comprehensive health assessment device.

Quick facts

  • Live on less than $2.50 a day.

  • More than ½ world’s population lacks access to essential health care.
